Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 — Effective immediately

Bravorik is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains clearly what data we collect, how we use it and your rights as a visitor. We believe in full transparency.

// 01

Who we are

Bravorik is a Tech and AI content platform founded by Soumodeep Patra in 2025. Our website is located at bravorik.github.io and our contact email is bravorik.business@gmail.com.

We create educational content about Artificial Intelligence, Technology and related topics for students and learners worldwide.

// 02

What information we collect

We collect minimal information to improve your experience on our website. This may include:

  • Basic analytics data — pages visited, time spent, device type and country via Google Analytics
  • Email address — only if you contact us directly via email
  • Comments — if you post a comment on our articles when the login system is active
  • Username and password — only if you create an account, stored encrypted and secure

We do not collect sensitive personal information like financial data, government IDs or medical information.

// 03

How we use your information

Any information collected is used only for the following purposes:

  • To understand how visitors use our website and improve our content
  • To respond to your messages or collaboration requests
  • To manage your account and comments on our platform
  • To send updates about new articles or content — only if you opt in

We will never sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes.

// 04

Cookies

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve your browsing experience. These include:

  • Analytics cookies — from Google Analytics to track page views and traffic
  • Session cookies — to keep you logged in if you have an account

You can disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. Note that some features may not work correctly if cookies are disabled.

// 05

Third party services

Bravorik uses the following third party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics — for website traffic analysis
  • YouTube — for embedded video content
  • Instagram — for social media links
  • GitHub Pages — for website hosting

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these services separately.

// 06

Your rights

As a visitor or user of Bravorik, you have the following rights:

  • The right to know what personal data we hold about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data at any time
  • The right to opt out of analytics tracking
  • The right to update or correct your account information

To exercise any of these rights, email us and we will respond within 7 working days.

// 07

Children's privacy

Bravorik's content is designed for students and learners of all ages. However, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

// 08

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as our platform grows. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top. We encourage you to check this page periodically.

// 09

Contact us

If you have any questions, concerns or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please reach out to us:

bravorik.business@gmail.com

We are committed to responding to all privacy related queries within 7 working days.
