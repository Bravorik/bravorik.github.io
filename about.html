Our Story

The story behind Bravorik

A passionate creator on a mission to make Artificial Intelligence and Technology simple, exciting and accessible for every student and learner out there.

Roger Becker

Founder and Creator — Bravorik

Roger Becker is a passionate tech content creator and digital storyteller with over 8 years of experience in the technology and videography industry. What started as a curiosity for cameras and computers evolved into a full-fledged mission — making complex technology understandable for everyone. Roger founded Bravorik in 2025 with one simple goal: bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI and everyday learners.

The Journey

How Bravorik began

It all started with a simple frustration — every time Roger searched for AI and tech content, the explanations were either too complicated for beginners or too shallow to actually learn from. There was a massive gap between what experts were saying and what everyday students and learners actually needed.

With 8 years of hands-on experience in technology and videography, Roger decided it was time to build something different. Something real. Something that speaks the language of students — not professors. And so, in 2025, Bravorik was born.

The name Bravorik represents boldness, creativity and the relentless pursuit of knowledge. It is not just a brand — it is a movement for the next generation of tech-savvy creators, students and dreamers.
